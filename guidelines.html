Community Guidelines

KUDASAI is built for connection, creativity, and expression. These guidelines protect that space for everyone. They apply to all users, all content, and all interactions.

🌟 Our Core Principle

KUDASAI is a platform for building the future together. We expect every member to treat others with dignity, engage in good faith, and contribute positively to the community. This isn't just a rule — it's what makes the platform worth being a part of.

💙

Be Kind & Respectful

Treat every user with dignity. Healthy debate is welcome — personal attacks are not. Disagree with ideas, not people.

🚫

No Harassment

Harassment, bullying, threats, and targeted abuse are strictly prohibited. This includes doxxing, sustained attacks, and coordinated pile-ons.

🗑️

No Spam

Do not post repetitive content, flood feeds with bot-generated posts, or send unsolicited DM promotions. Quality over quantity.

⛔

No Hate Speech

Content that attacks people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality is not allowed.

🔞

NSFW Policy

Explicit sexual content is not permitted. Suggestive content must be clearly marked. No explicit content involving minors under any circumstances — this results in permanent ban and reporting to authorities.

🎭

No Impersonation

Do not impersonate other users, public figures, or the KUDASAI team. Fan accounts are allowed if clearly labeled.

©️

Respect Copyright

Only post content you own or have rights to share. Don't repost others' artwork, music, or videos without credit or permission.

🦠

No Malware or Phishing

Never share links or files containing malware, viruses, or designed to steal credentials. This is an immediate permanent ban.

📰

No Misinformation

Deliberately spreading false information — especially about health, safety, or elections — is prohibited. We encourage citing sources for significant claims.

🎨

Celebrate Creativity

KUDASAI thrives on creative content — art, writing, music, ideas. Share what you make and celebrate what others make.

🤝

Support Each Other

Lift others up. Engage authentically. Comment constructively. Reactions and threads should build conversations, not tear them down.

🤖

No Automation Abuse

Bots, scrapers, and auto-posting scripts are not permitted without express approval. Don't manipulate engagement metrics artificially.

⚖️ Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Content removal — the offending post or message is deleted
  • Temporary suspension — account locked for 24h to 30 days depending on severity
  • Permanent ban — for severe or repeat violations
  • Legal reporting — for illegal content (CSAM, threats, cybercrime)

We enforce these guidelines consistently and without bias. Appeals can be submitted to blessmall212@gmail.com.

🚨 Report a Violation

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately. Include the username, post link, and description of the violation.

✉️ blessmall212@gmail.com

We review all reports and respond within 48 hours for urgent safety issues.
