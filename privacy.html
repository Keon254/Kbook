Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026. This policy explains how KUDASAI collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

📋 Overview

KUDASAI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect and use information when you use our platform at kudasai.replit.app. By using KUDASAI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📥 Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and the username you choose. This is used to create and identify your account.

Content You Post: Text posts, threads, poll responses, images, videos, and audio files you upload are stored on our servers (via Supabase) and may be visible to other users.

Messages: Direct messages between users are stored securely. We do not read private messages except when required to investigate abuse reports.

Usage Data: We may collect basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited to improve the platform's performance and security.

🔐 Authentication

Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth, which supports email/password login and Google OAuth. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard encryption. If you sign in with Google, we only receive your email address and public profile name from Google.

🗄️ Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure, which is hosted on AWS (Amazon Web Services). Supabase complies with SOC 2 Type II and GDPR standards. Files you upload (images, audio, video) are stored in Supabase Storage with secure access controls.

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

KUDASAI uses browser local storage to save your preferences (such as dark/light mode and draft posts). We may use cookies for session management via Supabase Auth. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

🔄 How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your posts, profile, and interactions to other users
  • To send you notifications about activity on your content
  • To improve platform performance and fix bugs
  • To enforce our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
  • To respond to support requests sent to our email

🤝 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Google OAuth — optional login method (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography (Inter font family)

We do not sell your data to any third parties.

🛡️ Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Database access is restricted by Row Level Security (RLS) policies — users can only access their own private data.

🗑️ Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at blessmall212@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

👶 Children's Privacy

KUDASAI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years of age. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
