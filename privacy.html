📋 1. Overview

KUDASAI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our platform at kudasai.replit.app.

By using KUDASAI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

We are not in the business of selling your data. We collect only what we need to operate the Platform, and we give you control over your information.

📥 2. Data We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and the username you choose. This is the minimum required to create and manage your account.

Profile Information: Your public profile may include your username, display name, bio, avatar, and any other information you voluntarily add. This information is visible to other users.

Content You Post: Text posts, threads, replies, poll responses, image URLs, and any other content you create on the Platform is stored in our database and may be visible to other users based on your privacy settings.

Direct Messages: Private messages between users are stored securely. We do not read private messages except when required to investigate abuse reports or comply with legal obligations.

Interactions: We record your likes, reposts, bookmarks, follows, and poll votes to provide the Platform's core functionality.

Technical Data: We may collect basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited to improve Platform performance and security. This data is not linked to your identity for advertising purposes.

We do not collect your real name, phone number, payment information, or physical address unless you voluntarily provide it.

🔄 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect exclusively to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Display your posts, profile, and interactions to other users
  • Deliver real-time features (feeds, notifications, messages)
  • Improve Platform performance and fix bugs
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Respond to your support requests or abuse reports
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the Platform
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your data to show you personalized ads, build advertising profiles, or sell it to third parties.

🔐 4. Authentication

Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth, which supports email/password login and Google OAuth ("Sign in with Google").

  • Email/Password: Passwords are never stored in plain text. They are hashed using bcrypt, an industry-standard cryptographic algorithm.
  • Google OAuth: If you sign in with Google, we only receive your email address and public profile name from Google. We do not receive your Google contacts, calendar, or any other Google data.
  • Password Reset: If you request a password reset, we will send a secure one-time link to your email. This link expires after a short time.
Your session is maintained via a secure HTTP-only cookie managed by Supabase Auth. We do not store raw tokens in your browser's local storage.

🗄️ 5. Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure, hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS). Supabase complies with industry standards including:

  • SOC 2 Type II certification
  • GDPR compliance for EU users
  • Encryption at rest for all stored data
  • Encryption in transit via HTTPS/TLS 1.2+

Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies — meaning each user can only access their own private data. Other users cannot read your private messages, draft posts, or account settings.

🍪 6. Cookies & Local Storage

KUDASAI uses the following browser storage mechanisms:

  • Session cookies: Set by Supabase Auth to keep you signed in securely
  • Local storage: Used to save your preferences (dark/light mode, draft posts)

We do not use:

  • Third-party advertising cookies
  • Tracking pixels or web beacons
  • Cross-site tracking technologies

You can clear cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings, though this will sign you out and reset your preferences.

🤐 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service providers: Only with Supabase (our infrastructure provider) who process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or government request, we may disclose data to the extent legally required
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or security of KUDASAI, our users, or the public
We will never voluntarily sell your data to advertisers, data brokers, or any commercial third parties.

🤝 8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Platform:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, real-time subscriptions, and file storage. Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google OAuth — Optional sign-in method. Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google Fonts — Inter font family for typography. Google may log your IP when loading fonts. About ↗
  • Replit — Platform hosting infrastructure. Privacy Policy ↗

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to read them to understand how they handle your data.

🛡️ 9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies
  • We never store your raw authentication tokens in insecure locations
  • Our Supabase infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II certified
No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to blessmall212@gmail.com rather than disclosing it publicly.

🗓️ 10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Platform's services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained until you request deletion
  • Posts and content: Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Messages: Retained until deleted by either party or account closure
  • Technical logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes

After account deletion, residual copies of your data may remain in our backup systems for a limited time before being overwritten.

🗑️ 11. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time (by closing your account)
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at blessmall212@gmail.com. We will respond to all verified requests within 30 days.

If you are an EU resident, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

👶 12. Children's Privacy

KUDASAI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account or provided personal information, please contact us immediately at blessmall212@gmail.com. We will promptly delete the account and all associated data.

🔁 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify logged-in users through the Platform for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Policy periodically. Continued use of the Platform after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
